## 2. Mission & one-liners

**Mission:** help people in Egypt feel calmer, one squeeze at a time. We sell exactly one
thing — squishy stress toys, in three sizes — and everything about the brand (colors, copy,
packaging, content) should lower the customer's heart rate, not raise it.

- **EN tagline:** *Squeeze the stress away.*
- **AR tagline:** *فرّغ توترك… بسكوشة*
- **Meta description:** "Squishy stress toys in Egypt — small, medium & jumbo slow-rising
  squishies that help you relax, focus, and unwind. Cash on delivery & fast shipping."

## 3. Calm positioning (the brand's core — research-backed)

Sqoosh is a **calm/wellness brand that happens to sell toys**, not a toy shop. The market is
moving the same way: successful fidget/sensory brands position as everyday wellness gear for
stress and focus, not kids' novelties.

**What the science supports (use these angles in content):**

- Repetitive squeezing engages the "rest and digest" (parasympathetic) response and gives
  nervous energy a physical outlet — a simple, real de-stress mechanism.
- The tactile, grounding sensation redirects attention away from anxious thoughts to the
  present moment (classic grounding technique).
- Studies on stress balls / haptic squeezing show reduced situational anxiety and arousal —
  helpful for exams, meetings, commutes, doomscrolling breaks.

**Content pillars built on this:**

| Pillar | Example content |
| --- | --- |
| Micro-calm rituals | "3 squeezes before you open your email" · slow-rising breathing: press in as you inhale, release as it rises |
| Situational relief | exam season kits, meeting-safe small squishies, bedside jumbo for winding down |
| Satisfying sensory | ASMR squish videos, glow-in-the-dark night routines |
| Community calm | customer "my desk sqoosh" photos, calm-corner setups |

**Honest-claims rule (non-negotiable):** squishies help with *everyday* stress and restlessness.
We never claim to treat anxiety disorders, ADHD, or any medical condition, and never use
medical language in product copy ("relieves anxiety disorder" ❌ · "helps you unwind" ✅).
Calm brand = trustworthy brand.

## 4. Personality & voice

| Trait | Do | Don't |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Calming** | Slow, reassuring rhythm; short sentences; whitespace | Shouty caps, urgency spam, countdown pressure |
| **Playful** | Puns, sound words ("sqoosh!"), light emoji in social | Corporate tone, luxury language ("curated", "elegant") |
| **Warm & safe** | Clear COD/shipping facts near the buy button | Hype pressure, fake scarcity |
| **Satisfying** | Sensory words: soft, slow-rising, squish, sink, breathe | Technical jargon (PU foam, TPR) in customer copy |
| **Egyptian** | Casual Egyptian Arabic in social & WhatsApp | Stiff MSA in marketing (fine for legal pages) |

Voice examples:
- Add-to-cart success: "In the bag 🫧 breathe out."
- Free-shipping bar: "قربت! Add 120 EGP for free shipping."
- Sold out: "Squished too hard — back soon. Pre-order below."
- Product line: "Slow-rising. Like you after a long day — give it a second, it bounces back."

## 5. Logo direction (for the designer / future task)

- Wordmark "sqoosh" in a rounded, chubby lowercase font — letters look slightly squeezed
  (the two o's can be squished ovals, like a pressed squishy).
- Optional mascot: a round, sleepy-calm blob face ("Sqooshy") for stickers, favicons, 404 pages.
- Works in one color (calm teal on cream, or white on teal). Must read at sticker size (3 cm).
- Gender-neutral by design — see `color-styling-guide.md` §1 (Ocean Calm palette).

## 7. Packaging (part of the calm ritual)

- Teal or sand/cream mailer with the wordmark + a sticker sheet (mascot + "sqoosh it!").
- Thank-you card, two sides: referral code + "post your squish, tag @sqoosh.eg" on one side;
  a **60-second calm ritual** on the other (breathe-with-the-squishy instructions) — on-brand,
  shareable, and it teaches the product's real use.
- A free tiny sticker in every order is the signature touch. Fun over fancy.
